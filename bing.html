    Anthony Norman Albanese is an Australian politician who is the 31st and current prime minister of Australia since 2022. He has been the leader of the Labor Party since 2019 and a member of parliament for the New South Wales division of Grayndler since 1996. Albanese previously served as the 15th deputy prime minister under the second Rudd government in 2013. He held various ministerial positions from 2007 to 2013 in the governments of Kevin Rudd and Julia Gillard.
